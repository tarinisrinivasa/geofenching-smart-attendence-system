1. System Overview & Compliance Mandate

GeoAttend is an enterprise educational attendance automation and perimeter security platform. By accessing or utilizing this portal, you explicitly consent to institutional regulations, automated attendance monitoring, and these Terms of Service.

2. Geolocation & Real-Time Perimeter Tracking

To automate class check-ins and maintain campus perimeter safety, GeoAttend collects and processes your mobile device's precise GPS telemetry (latitude, longitude, and accuracy radius) during active class sessions and institutional operating hours configured by college administration.

  • GPS coordinates are evaluated strictly against designated classroom and campus geofences.
  • Tracking is automatically restricted outside institutional operating hours unless explicitly enabled by college rules.

3. Hardware ID Binding & Biometric Verification

To eliminate proxy attendance, account sharing, and credential theft:

  • Your account is cryptographically bound to your specific physical device hardware identifier (`device_id`).
  • Student check-ins require WebAuthn biometric authentication (Fingerprint or Face ID) registered on your bound device.
  • Attempting login from an unregistered or unverified device triggers an immediate security alert to HOD administration.

4. Automated Attendance Suspensions & Parent SMS Alerts

⚠️ Mandatory Institutional Regulation:
1. Exceeding campus or classroom perimeters during an active class session flags an unauthorized perimeter breach.
2. Accumulating more than 2 unexcused absences (3 or more missed sessions) will automatically suspend your attendance privileges and dispatch an automated SMS alert to your registered parent/guardian phone number.

5. Telemetry & Data Encryption

System telemetry—including battery status, connection health, and security event logs—is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS 1.3) and stored in secure institutional databases strictly for academic monitoring and student safety.

6. User Consent & Contact

Acceptance of this Privacy Policy & Terms of Service is mandatory to access the GeoAttend login portal. If you have questions regarding data privacy or device binding resets, please contact your institutional HOD or System Administrator.
